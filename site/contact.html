FAQ

Common questions.

How do you price projects?

Project-based, scoped upfront. We don't do open-ended retainers. After discovery, you get a clear proposal with a fixed scope and price. Changes in scope are discussed and agreed before work starts — not billed as surprises after.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes — if the problem is specific and the timeline is realistic. We don't work well with "we'll figure out what we want as we go." But if you know the problem you're solving and need a technical partner who moves fast, we're a good fit.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely. We often integrate with in-house developers, product managers, and domain experts. We're not a black box — we document everything, explain our decisions, and make sure your team can own the output.

What industries do you work in?

We're technology-generalists, not industry specialists. We've built for services, manufacturing, legal, and e-commerce. The common thread is always a concrete problem that technology can solve — not a vague desire to "do AI."
