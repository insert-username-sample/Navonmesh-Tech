Case Study 01

SwiftWash:
Hyperlocal Supply Chain.

A premium laundry quick-commerce ecosystem built from the ground up—including customer apps, operator CRMs, and intelligent logistics orchestration.

CategoryQuick Commerce
StackFlutter, Node.js

The Core Problem

The Indian laundry sector is highly fragmented, with millions of SMEs operating without digital infrastructure. Traditional models lead to delays, inventory loss, and a lack of transparency for customers used to quick-commerce standards set by Zepto and Blinkit.

Our Solution

SwiftWash is an end-to-end "Supply Chain as a Service" for laundry. We didn't just build an app; we built an operating system for laundry shops.

The platform includes:
• Customer App: UI/UX centered on speed—order to pickup in under 4 minutes.
• Operator App: A custom CRM that manages inventory, pricing, and revenue in real-time.
• Driver App: Real-time route optimization for hyper-local delivery.

Impact & Architecture

By implementing a "Node-Based" logistics strategy, we turn individual laundry shops into localized distribution hubs. This architecture removes the need for centralized warehouses (dark stores), significantly lowering CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) while maintaining 2-hour turnaround times.
