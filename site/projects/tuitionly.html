Case Study 06

Tuitionly:
The Student OS.

A student-centric academic operating system that syncs schedules, resources, and performance tracking into a single, intelligent interface.

CategoryEdTech
StackReact, Supabase

The Core Problem

Traditional EdTech remains siloed—fragmented between LMS portals, static textbook PDFs, and passive video content. The cognitive load of managing the "infrastructure" of learning often detracts from the learning itself.

Our Solution: OpenMAIC

Tuitionly evolved from a private venture into the **OpenMAIC (Multi-Agent AI Classroom)** research project. It acts as a unified autonomous layer that doesn't just store data; it optimizes it.

Advanced Reasoning: MiroThinker

Integrated at the core is the **MiroThinker** reasoning agent. This system utilizes "interaction scaling"—high-frequency environment feedback loops that allow the AI to self-correct complex logic chains during session simulations.

Technical Implementation

  • PDF-to-Curriculum: Automated extraction pipelines converting raw academic documents into structured, multi-modal project-based learning (PBL) simulations.
  • Agentic Feedback: Real-time self-correction loops utilizing interaction scaling to verify reasoning benchmarks against ground-truth data.
