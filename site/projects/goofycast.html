Case Study 17

GoofyCast:
Building in Public.

A content distribution platform designed for technical storytelling and creative documentation of hardware and agentic builds.

CategoryDistribution
FocusTechnical Narrative

The Core Problem

Most hard-tech innovation happens in silence. Builders are so focused on the hardware that the "story" is forgotten, making it difficult for the public or collaborators to understand the impact of the build.

Technical Execution

GoofyCast is our interface for the world. We don't just "show" the product; we document the failure, the architecture, and the outcome. It's a high-fidelity distribution system that uses cinematic technical storytelling to turn complex engineering into high-signal content.
