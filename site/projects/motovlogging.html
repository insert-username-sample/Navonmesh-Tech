Case Study 18

Moto OS:
Ride Intelligent.

A specialized software stack for motovlogging, integrating camera control, GPS, and telemetry into a single, voice-controlled HUD.

CategoryMobile Systems
StackAndroid (Custom), BLE

The Core Problem

Motovlogging requires balancing riding safety with complex equipment management—cameras, audio recorders, and GPS. Traditional setups are distracting and prone to failure mid-ride.

Technical Execution

Moto OS is a custom Android integration that centralizes all ride-critical data. Using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), it syncs with external cameras and sensors, providing a simplified voice-activated HUD that lets the rider focus on the road while the OS manages the recording.
