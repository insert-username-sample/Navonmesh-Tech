Enterprise Compute: Sovereign AI Infrastructure.

Custom-built, high-density GPU clusters designed for organizations requiring absolute data sovereignty and dedicated compute power.

CategoryTechnology
StackCustom Engine

The Core Problem

As foundation models become core business assets, relying on public cloud providers creates significant risks: data leaks via inference, unpredictable quota limits, and massive egress costs. For frontier research, "standard" cloud instances often lack the optimized interconnects and thermal stability required for long-running training jobs.

Our Solution

We architect and deploy private, in-house GPU clusters. These aren't just "servers in a rack"—they are cohesive systems featuring high-speed InfiniBand networking, custom liquid or high-airflow cooling, and air-gapped security protocols.

Navonmesh provides the full stack: from hardware selection and supply chain management to the deployment of software layers like Slurm, Kubernetes, and optimized CUDA environments.

Impact & Architecture

By bringing compute in-house, our clients achieve up to 60% reduction in long-term TCO compared to cloud reservations, while maintaining 100% control over their weights and proprietary data. Our "Secure Link" architecture allows for hybrid setups where inference happens locally while less sensitive pre-processing scale to the cloud.
