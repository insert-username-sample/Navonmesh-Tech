Case Study 02

Clausely:
Deterministic Law.

AI-First legal compliance platform for India. Automating clause analysis, policy generation, and multi-jurisdiction safety.

CategoryAI SaaS
StackNext.js, LangChain

The Core Problem

Legal compliance in India is a bottleneck for SMEs. Traditional legal services are slow, expensive, and non-deterministic. Generative AI tools often hallucinate legal clauses, creating massive liability risks.

The Clausely Engine

Clausely uses a Deterministic Retrieval architecture. Instead of letting the AI write law, we use it to map your business needs to verified, lawyer-approved legal templates. We solve for accuracy first, then efficiency.
