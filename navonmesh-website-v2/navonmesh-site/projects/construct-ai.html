Case Study 23

Construct AI:
The Hub.

An enterprise-grade estimation console that converts complex analog BOQs and blueprints into structured digital data in seconds using Gemini 3 OCR.

CategoryEnterprise AI / Civil
StackGemini 3 Pro, Firebase, Three.js

The Analog Workflow Problem

The construction industry runs on paper and legacy PDFs. Bill of Quantities (BOQs) are often thousands of lines long, hidden in sub-optimal analog formats that resist automation. This leads to massive estimation errors and multi-million dollar delays.

The Gemini-Powered Solution

Construct AI uses a custom OCR layer built on Gemini 3 Pro to ingest heterogeneous documents—from hand-sketched blueprints to scanned bills. It doesn't just read text; it understands the semantic relationship between materials, costs, and project timelines.

Interactive Estimation Console

Built with a high-fidelity 3D visualization core (Three.js), the platform allows project managers to "see" their budget distribution in real-time as the AI digitizes the project scope.
