Almost Alien:
Wearable Frontier.

An experimental high-fashion and technology brand exploring the intersection of wearable hardware and futuristic aesthetics.

CategoryFashion Tech

The Thesis

Technology is becoming invisible, but it's also becoming intimate. Almost Alien is a playground for "Trans-humanist Design"—clothing and accessories that incorporate smart textiles, haptic feedback, and biomechanical structures.

Technical Execution

Using procedural geometry and computational design, we create silhouettes that would be impossible to manufacture with traditional methods. Every piece is a conversation between human anatomy and machine intelligence.
