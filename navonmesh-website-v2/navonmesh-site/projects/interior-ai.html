Case Study 11

Interior AI:
Spatial Design.

Real-time 3D interior design using spatial mapping and generative AI to visualize floor plans from simple mobile scans.

CategorySpatial Computing
StackThree.js, WebGL

The Core Problem

Architects and interior designers spend days creating 3D models from 2D measurements. For clients, visualizing a finished space from a blueprint is nearly impossible without expensive, high-poly renders.

The Spatial Pipeline

We built a pipeline that turns a smartphone "scan" into a semantically labeled 3D mesh. The AI then suggests textures, furniture layouts, and lighting—allowing for real-time iteration directly in the browser.
